About Me

Musa Aktaş, at the age of 19, stands as the visionary CEO of Whales Company, a leader in renewable energy solutions. Despite his young age, Musa brings a fresh perspective and a relentless drive for innovation to the company. Under his leadership, Whales Company has embraced cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices. Musa's passion for environmental conservation has been instrumental in shaping the company's strategic direction towards achieving carbon neutrality by 2030. Outside of his professional endeavors, Musa is known for his interest in marine biology, which reflects his commitment to protecting marine ecosystems. He actively participates in local beach clean-up initiatives, demonstrating his dedication to making a positive impact beyond the business world. Musa Aktaş embodies a leadership style characterized by empathy and collaboration, fostering a culture of inclusivity and continuous improvement within Whales Company. His goal is to steer Whales Company towards becoming a global leader in sustainable energy solutions, ensuring a brighter future for generations to come.
